Your creator code is personal to you and non-transferable: you may not sell it, lend it, or let anyone else operate it. Whether a code carries a buyer discount, and at what rate, is set per code and may differ between creators. We and Tebex may modify, suspend, or deactivate any code at any time, including to correct errors, respond to abuse, or change the program. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of changes that affect your code.
