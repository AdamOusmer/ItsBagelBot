---
heading: "Refunds, Chargebacks & Adjustments"
plain: "If a purchase is refunded or charged back, the matching share is reversed."
---

If a purchase attributed to your code is refunded, reversed, or charged back, the corresponding revenue share is reversed as well, and may be deducted from pending or future payouts in accordance with Tebex's processes. We may also withhold or reverse amounts that we or Tebex reasonably determine result from fraud, self-purchasing, or other breaches of these terms.
