To receive a creator code, you must apply and be approved. Approval, and the terms attached to any individual code, are at our sole discretion. To participate you must:

- Have an ItsBagelBot account in good standing and comply with the Terms of Service and Twitch's Terms of Service
- Be of the age of majority in your jurisdiction, since payouts require the capacity to enter a contract
- Meet any account or verification requirements Tebex imposes for receiving payouts

We may decline any application without giving a reason.
