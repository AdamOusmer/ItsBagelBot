---
heading: "Creator Conduct"
plain: "Promote honestly, disclose that you earn from your code, and don't game the system."
---

As a participating creator, you must not:

- Use your own code, or arrange purchases through intermediaries, to earn a share on your own spending
- Make false or misleading claims about ItsBagelBot, the discount, or your relationship with us
- Promote your code through spam, unsolicited messages, or paid ads on our name or trademarks
- Encourage purchases you know are likely to be refunded or charged back

You must clearly disclose, where your code is promoted, that you earn from purchases made with it, as required by applicable advertising and consumer protection rules.
