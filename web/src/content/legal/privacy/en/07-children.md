ItsBagelBot is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact [support@itsbagelbot.com](mailto:support@itsbagelbot.com) and we will delete it.
