---
heading: "Your Rights (GDPR, PIPEDA, Law 25, CCPA)"
plain: "Ask for your data, portability, or its deletion any time. California residents get CCPA rights too. You can also complain to a privacy regulator."
---

Under GDPR, Law 25, and PIPEDA, you have the right to access, rectify, port, or erase your personal data. You can request data export or deletion at any time by contacting [support@itsbagelbot.com](mailto:support@itsbagelbot.com). Deleting your account removes all associated data immediately, except for minimal records we are legally required to keep, such as transaction audit logs.

**California residents:** under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA, as amended by the CPRA), you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to access it, to correct it, to delete it, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell your personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioural advertising, so there is nothing to opt out of. You can exercise these rights, yourself or through an authorized agent, by contacting [support@itsbagelbot.com](mailto:support@itsbagelbot.com); we will verify your request and respond within the timelines the CCPA requires.

If you believe we have not handled your personal data properly, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority: the Commission d'accès à l'information in Quebec, the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, your local data protection authority in the EU/EEA, or the California Privacy Protection Agency.
